Problem of interest:
Dots-And-Boxes is a game played between two people. The two players take turns drawing horizontal or vertical lines between adjacent points. If a player's move connects four dots as a square, then that player captures that square, and gets to make another move. Whoever captures the most squares wins!

P.E.A.S
Performance measure:
 Being able to acquire the max no. of boxes without conceding too many boxes to the user
Environment:
A 4x4 or 5x5 grid of dots
Actuators:
Mouse
